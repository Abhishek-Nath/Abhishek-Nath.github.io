What are e-Sports?

"e-Sports" is simply the short name for electronic sports.
Just like football players play football together, eSports players play computer games against each other.
Jaw-dropping cash prizes have been introduced, official leagues have been established and huge multinationals
have stepped in to offer generous sponsorship deals.
